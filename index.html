About

I'm a coder journalist with a passion for creating user-centered experiences, beautiful data visualizations and interactive stories. I work as the data editor at U.S. News & World Report where I lead the data journalism and graphics efforts in the newsroom. I also adjunct at American University, where I teach a class on data visualization.

Health Care Index

Designed and developed a template for USN index projects that allows for quick creation of exploratory data dashboards in a new full-width CMS template. Worked with multiple stakeholders across the IT, Design, Data and News departments to transform projects once communicated with a simple one-line Excel chart into an immersive D3 visualization that allows the user to explore thousands of raw and indexed data points.

  • Launched: May 2015
  • Skills: D3, HTML, CSS, JS, Data Cleaning

The Chase

A hub for U.S. News election content that ranks candidates based on polling data, records campaign trips to early primary states and uses a treemap of the candidates as navigation. The project is iterative — I'm in constant discussions with our editors and political team about how to make the project more useful to our readers.

  • Launched: July 2015
  • Skills: D3, Javascript, HTML/CSS, Product Management

The Toll of Hurricane Katrina

For the anniversary of Hurricane Katrina, we looked at the uncertain death toll of the storm. The visualization uses vertical scroll to communicate the historical toll of the event in Louisiana.

  • Launched: August 2015
  • Skills: HTML, CSS, Javascript

Trump Wealth Calculator

This calculator gives readers a dose of depression by allowing them to enter their net worths and receive real world comparisons to the Republican frontrunner's immense wealth.

  • Launced: September 2015
  • Skills: HTML, CSS, Javascript

Campaign Graveyard

With a crowded 2016 campaign field, many are bound to be disappointed. The graveyard allows readers to leave 2016 campaign buttons for their favorite ex-candidates. The graphic uses Google Forms and Tabletop to store user input.

  • Launched: December 2015
  • Skills: Javascript, HTML, CSS

1033 Program Data

Worked with USN political reporter on analysis of 1033 program data on release of military equipment, finding that much of the equipment went to the border with Mexico. Also wrote stories on the quality of the data, which states use the program more and non-military items in the program.

  • Launched: August 2014
  • Skills: Data Cleaning, Tableau

AP-Google Research

Created IRB-approved research project as an AP-Google Scholar through ONA. The project surveyed journalism and computer science students to determine what barriers existed between the departments. In addition to a post in the Source targeted to the journalism community, I co-authored an academic paper on lessons learned in the CS/journalism course I co-designed and co-taught, presented a report to the diversity committee of UGA's computer science department with recommendations and gave a TEDx talk about the confidence gap and how my attitude toward feminism changed as a result of my research.

  • Launched: June 2015
  • Skills: D3, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Data Cleaning

Mobile Prototypes
